Getting Started

Starter Kit​

Use the Shop Menu to claim one of your free Starter Kits. You will receive a few tools and tames to help you with harvesting and processing basic resources.

Kit Contents

  • Spitfire Pick and Spitfire Hatchet
    Improved versions of their vanilla counterparts that can be upgraded (See Upgrading Spitfire Weapons/Tools)
  • Awesome Spyglass
    See creature stats and Outlines
  • Soul Traps
    Improved version of a Cryopod
  • Eternal Ankylo (Saddle Provided)
    This dino harvests mod resources, when saddle is equiped it will act as a forge/cooker within its inventory.
  • Eternal Doedicurus (Saddle Provided)
    This dino harvests mod resources, when saddle is equiped it will act as a Morter and Pestle within its inventory.
  • Eternal Stegosaurus (Saddle Provided)
    Harvests additional resource types when gathering resources.
  • Vanilla Moth (Saddle Provided)
    Early game flying transport
  • Vanilla Ankylosaurus (Saddle Provided)
    Early game metal farming.

Useful Items​

We've listed a few useful items that are key for getting started early on.

  • Eternal Corpse Recovery
    You are limited to 1 of these per map, so ensure you pick it up after using it (Unless its in your base of course)
Eternal Corpse Recovery

Place these onto a thatch foundation so that it decays. This saves you remembering the original location to pick it up each time.

  • Awesome Teleporter + Remote
    You/Your tribe is limited to 5 of these Awesome Teleporter Pads per map.

  • Spitfire Armory
    These are the strongest weapons in the game, an electric spear or pike, will stun most standard Primal Fear and Eternal Dinos, allowing you to kill them easily early on.

  • Upgrade Station
    These structures allow you to max out durability and damage on weapons (Upgrade your Spitfire weapons to the highest tier first to get the most damage).

  • Spitfire Crops
    These Crop plots are essential to mass producing resources required on a 1000x Server. The T2 Plot is able to duplicate most items (Including Soul Trapped Dinos).

  • Eternal Glider
    This is cheap to make and can be attached to most chest armors in the game, allowing for easy early game flight.

  • Alpha Wyvern Saddle
    This saddle will work on nearly every dinosaur in the game.

Alpha Wyvern Saddle Bug

An Eternal Dev bug makes this uncraftable.
Buy from Ingame Shop (F2) - Then use the Upgrade Station to get a Max Armor BP to make more.
