Spitfire Weapons & Tools

Weapon / Tool Parts​

Infinite Weapon Part Crafting

How to BP for Infinite Crafts

Weapons parts and attachments can be found in Beaver Dams, Supply Drops, and Loot Crates (in caves/ocean).
Some common parts can also be purchased from the shop (F2).

Adding Attachments to Weapons​

  1. Place the Weapon and desired attachment into the Spitfire Armory.
  2. Select the Weapon and Click Add Attachment.

How to Upgrade a Spitfire Weapon / Tool​

tip

Use the Upgrade Station to create a blueprint of your upgraded weapon incase you lose it.

By default a weapon is Tier 1. All weapons can be upgraded to Tier 4, with some having a Legendary Tier.

  1. Place the Weapon/Tool and required parts into the Spitfire Armory
  2. Select the Weapon/Tool and click Upgrade Weapon

You can repeatedly click Upgrade Weapon to get through the tiers quickly, if you do no have the required Parts you will see a message advising this.
